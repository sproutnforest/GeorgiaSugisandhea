This website is my Frontend Programming class finals project in my third semester in university, where we are assigned to make a fullstack website with a team. So me and my team decided to make a website called MATCHI, which is a fashion exploring website that allows users to explore fashion items, comment on items, create collections for their fashion items to keep track of certain style or outfit, explore fashion trends and events, post blogs on fashions, as well as exploring clothing materials and how to take care of them.
