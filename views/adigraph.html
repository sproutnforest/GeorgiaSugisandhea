Third place adigraph poster competition

23 February 2024

I joined this competition with 2 of my close friends. This competition is about creating a poster with the theme of elections as a pillar of democracy in Indonesia. So we chose to research and create a poster about abstain voting, which is a common problem in Indonesia. We became the finalist and were called to do presentation on our research and poster. And finally, we won the third place in this competition. This is my first time joining a competition like this, and I am very happy to win it.
