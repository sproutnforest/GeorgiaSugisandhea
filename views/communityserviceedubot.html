Assistant for Community Service Program - Edubot13

2025

This community service program I was in is about Edubot13, an app made to help our team to collect data from teachers in a school to be used to train models to create a chatbot specifically made for elementary school students. In the team, I was the software engineer, specifically in database and frontend developing. In the process, we also went to an elementary school in Indonesia to train the teachers how to use the website to input their datas for us to use.
