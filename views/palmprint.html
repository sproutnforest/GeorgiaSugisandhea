Palmprint Biometric Detection

Researcher

2025

This is a machine learning paper that I made to compare and find the best machine learning methods to detect the correct identity owner from palmprint biometric using GLCM-Naive Bayes, GLCM-Decision Tree, Xception, Resnet101, and MobileNetV2. The result that I found was that the best method to use is Decision Tree, and the worst method to use is ResNet101. This paper was accepted and presented in 2025 International Conference on Advanced Technologies in Energy and Informatic (ICATEI).
